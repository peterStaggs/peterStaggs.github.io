The purpose of Whistler is to ensure family and friends know your whereabouts in the event you cannot access your phone. Users log in and create outing profiles that describe where they are going, with whom, etc. They then set a timer that will automatically send a text message to their emergency contact with pertinent information if the timer is not manually stopped. Note that users must remain logged in for the app to work. I built Whistler with Angular JS, the Twilio API, and Firebase for persisting data. The layout was made using the Angular Material service. This app is currently at early MVP status, but my plan is to refactor it with Ionic for a better mobile experience and deploy it.
