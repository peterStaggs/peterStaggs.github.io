"Card." is a hybrid mobile app for quickly storing business cards. The idea is for it to be simpler and faster than using LinkedIn, less storage-intensive than taking pictures of business cards, and of course, to not take up room in your wallet. Users fill out 7 lines of information to create their own cards, then add other users' cards to a list on their profile by simply entering an email. I built Card with inputs from a remote team in New York, which included copywriter Sam Bartos and designer Michael Ryterband. While the core functionality is in place, the app will continue to undergo development, with the eventual goal being a formal launch later this year. Card's backend is powered by a Django REST API which I built and hosted on Heroku. Everything client-facing was created with Ionic/Angular JS, and I used SASS for styling.
