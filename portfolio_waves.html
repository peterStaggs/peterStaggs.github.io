In Waves '87: Business Cruisin', you play the role of a down-and-out businessman in 1987 Southeast Asia who is found by pirates and given a second chance at life. I built this game with a team of six over a two day sprint for Global Game Jam 2017. We were instructed to create a game incorporating a main theme of "waves", and our resulting story combines hand-drawn Japanese woodblock-style art, 80s-esque synthesizer music, simple mini games, and a surreal narrative to create a playable homage to early Japanese animation films. We created all visual and audio elements on the spot. The code is vanilla Python with help from the Python-based RenPy library, which provides a framework for interactive novels.
